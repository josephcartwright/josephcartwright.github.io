Field Project
Our team was tasked with creating an AI powered solution that would scan incoming student emails in a professors inbox using NLP to gahter context, then respond to common and easily answered questions invlolving content from the class syllabus. If the AI was faced with a more complex or unrelated email, it would mark it as unread and flag it as important for the professor to answer themselves. This was aimed to eliminate a large potion of time dedicated by professors to responding to student emails while also organizing important messages.
